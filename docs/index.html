Core Features

Technology That Preserves Love

From memory collection to AI companionship, we use the warmest approach to preserve the most precious things.

Memory Collection

Upload text, photos, voice recordings, and videos. AI automatically organizes and analyzes them to build a complete digital memory archive.

Voice Cloning

With just a few voice samples, AI learns and clones your loved one's voice. Hear them speak again, reading words you want to hear.

AI Conversation

Based on your loved one's language patterns and memories, we create a warm AI companion. Share your day, hear their response.

Virtual Memorial

Create virtual memorial spaces with photos, candles, and flowers. On special days, come together to remember and honor.

Privacy Protection

All data is end-to-end encrypted, strictly following GDPR and data protection laws. Your memories belong only to you.

Family Sharing

Invite family members to contribute memories together. Support multi-role permission management for collaborative remembrance.
