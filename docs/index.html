FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the digital persona trained?

Based on text messages, voice recordings, and photos from your loved one, we use large language model fine-tuning and voice cloning technology to create a unique digital persona.

How is data privacy protected?

End-to-end encryption, user can delete all data at any time, never shared with third parties. We are HIPAA and GDPR compliant.

How much voice recording is needed?

We recommend 10-30 minutes of clear recordings. The more material provided, the more natural and accurate the persona will be.

Can I share the persona with family?

Yes, you can invite family members to interact with the persona. Each member gets their own private conversation space.
